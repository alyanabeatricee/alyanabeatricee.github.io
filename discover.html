The Eras Tour: A Celebration of Taylor Swift's Musical Journey

The Eras Tour is the ongoing sixth concert tour by Taylor Swift. It commenced on March 17, 2023, and will conclude on December 8, 2024. The tour spans five continents and consists of 149 shows, celebrating all of Swift's studio albums through a conceptually immersive experience.

The set list features over 40 songs, grouped into 10 acts that portray each album. In 2024, the tour was revamped to incorporate Swift's eleventh album, The Tortured Poets Department. Critics praised the tour for its production, aesthetics, and Swift's performance.

The Eras Tour has made a significant cultural and economic impact across the globe. It is the highest-grossing tour of all time, becoming the first in history to surpass US$1 billion in revenue.


Ticket Information

In 2023, Taylor Swift embarked on The Eras Tour, a monumental celebration of her entire musical catalog. Designed as a tribute to each "era" of her career, the tour took fans on a journey through time, highlighting every album and era Taylor has crafted since her debut.

The Eras Tour is a unique concert experience where Taylor performs songs from all 10 of her studio albums, each representing distinct phases of her career, from her country beginnings to her pop and beyond. Each section of the concert is dedicated to a specific album, with matching visuals, costumes, and set designs reflecting the mood, style, and themes of that album.

The tour set a new standard for live performances with its record-breaking ticket sales, immersive storytelling, and the sheer length of the shows.
